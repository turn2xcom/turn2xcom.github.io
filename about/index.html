2012

Pioneering Beginnings

At TURN2X, our journey began in 2012 at the Engler Bunte Institute at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), the same place where the Haber Bosch process was invented. Our CTO Dr. Dominik Schollenberger embarked on pioneering research in methanation and the engineering of chemical plants. This early work laid the foundation for our ambitious mission to reduce Europes dependence on fossil natural gas.

2021

The Founders

Equally important to our mission is the story of friendship and collaboration that brought us together. Philip, Benedikt, and Dominik all went to the KIT, where their shared vision and passion for sustainable energy ignited the idea for TURN2X.

2022

Born with a Vision

In October 2022, TURN2X was officially founded with a clear and bold missionto avoid 1 gigaton of CO2 emissions and displace 10% of Germany's fossil natural gas demand. This Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG) reflects our unwavering commitment to driving meaningful change in the energy sector.

2023

Driving Innovation

Our dedication to innovation and resilience was further recognized in 2023 when TURN2X became a part of the Energy Resilience Leadership Group (ERLG), an initiative born at the Munich Security Conference. As part of this group, we continue to foster collaboration and lead the charge in energy innovation.

Our Mission

There is no Planet B.
Our mission is to avoid 1 gigaton of CO2 emissions and displace 10% of Germany's fossil natural gas demand by pioneering innovative renewable natural gas solutions.

The Task Force

Working at TURN2X

TURN2X is powered by a growing team of over 20 passionate individuals, each bringing unique expertise and energy to our mission. While our headquarter is located in Munichs dynamic Werksviertel district, a vibrant startup ecosystem, we embrace a hybrid work model. Every month, our team gathers in Munich for strategy sessions, face-to-face collaboration, and offsite events that strengthen our bonds and drive our shared goals forward. This blend of flexibility and in-person connection ensures that we remain agile, innovative, and united in our pursuit of a sustainable energy future.

OUR VALUES

Earn Customer Love

Extreme Ownership

Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit
